Why tissue culture

The same reasons plantations across India have moved off seed & sucker propagation.

Uniform, predictable growth

Every plantlet is a genetic clone of a screened mother plant — no off-types in the field, no surprises at harvest.

Virus & pathogen-free

Lab propagation strips out the soil and sucker-borne diseases that quietly cut yield in conventionally raised stock.

Faster multiplication at scale

We can supply plantation-scale volumes on a fixed delivery calendar — not limited by how many suckers one field can spare.

Our process

From mother plant to your field, in four ringed stages.

Named after the culm — bamboo's own growth rings mark real, sequential stages, not decoration.

01

Mother plant selection

High-yield, disease-free mother plants are screened and indexed before any culturing begins.

02

In-vitro multiplication

Explants are multiplied in sterile lab culture, generation after generation, true to the source plant.

03

Hardening nursery

Plantlets move from the lab to a shaded, humidity-controlled nursery to build field resilience.

04

Field-ready dispatch

Only saplings that clear our survival checks are packed and dispatched to your plantation.
