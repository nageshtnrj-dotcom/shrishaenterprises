About Shrisha Enterprises

Agro traders focused on tissue-culture planting material.

What we supply

A focused catalog, built for fast decisions.

Shrisha Enterprises supplies tissue-culture bamboo and banana saplings for farmers, growers, and plantation projects across Karnataka.

The catalog is intentionally focused so enquiries can move quickly from crop choice to availability, quantity, and delivery planning.

Founder's expertise

Backed by agricultural expertise and decades of seed industry experience.

Shrisha Enterprises is led by an agricultural professional who is a Gold Medalist in M.Sc Agriculture and brings more than 25 years of experience in the seed industry. Her background gives the business a practical understanding of planting material, farmer expectations, crop establishment, and the discipline required for dependable agro inputs.

That experience now guides Shrisha's focused work with tissue-culture raised bamboo and banana saplings — helping growers make clearer variety choices and plan orders with confidence.
